
# Scene 7D: Finishing off the beast 

As Godzilla lays there defeated by you, you raise your blade preparing to finish him off... 

Months later and you are regarded as a hero, the one who defeated Godzilla.  Loved by all, you have a seemingly perfect life, money, fame, what else do you need? Although with all this you can’t help but feel something missing, a friend... a little guy perhaps?
