
# Scene 4D: Your Fate 

You get sent home for cheating. As you're sitting at home with your friends, all wallowing in your own self-pity, you see reports on the news about a Godzilla monster attack. This only makes all of you sadder that you couldn’t be there to experience it. Shortly thereafter, your home is crushed by Godzilla. 

Cheaters never prosper; I am disappointed in you.
