
# Scene 8B: The Final Fight    

Your efforts are not in vain, and The Pebble agrees to help. You all approach Godzilla geared up as much as possible. The fight goes on for hours at a complete stalemate. Godzilla attempts to charge a fatal blow to tip the scales only for The Pebble to use his secret weapon; The Can’t Lose Clause. The Pebble has it in his contract he cannot lose ever, so Godzilla's fatal blow fails, and the beast ends up falling in defeat. It’s over, you won.
