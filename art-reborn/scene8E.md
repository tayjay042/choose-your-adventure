
# Scene 8E: The Final Fight   

It's up to you and The Pebble to save the world. You combat the beast for countless hours going back and forth the whole time. Godzilla gets the upper hand and prepares to finish the fight. That's when The Pebble steps up and uses his secret weapon; The Can’t Lose Clause. The Pebble has it in his contract he cannot lose ever, so Godzilla's attempt to win fails, and the beast ends up falling in defeat. It’s over, you won.
